# 01. Jordan Algebras

A Jordan algebra over `k` is a commutative algebra satisfying

```text
(x^2 y) x = x^2 (y x).
```

In characteristic not equal to `2` and `3`, the identity can be replaced by a
multilinearized cubic identity. The operadic setup uses this multilinear form.
