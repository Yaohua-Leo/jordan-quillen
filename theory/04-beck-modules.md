# 04. Beck Modules

For Quillen-style cohomology, the decisive object is not just the absolute
abelianization of `J`, but the category of abelian group objects over `J`.

Expected picture:

- A `J`-module should correspond to a square-zero extension `J plus M`.
- The multiplication on `J plus M` should recover the Jordan module identities.
- Beck modules should provide the coefficient objects for cohomology.

## First Target

Derive the module identities by imposing the Jordan identity on a square-zero
extension and retaining terms linear in `M`.
