# 10. TKK Comparison

The Tits-Kantor-Koecher construction sends Jordan data to Lie-theoretic data.
It can produce useful computable invariants, but it is external to the Jordan
category.

Working comparison question:

```text
How different is H_*^Jord(J) from H_*^Lie(TKK(J))?
```

Initial expectation: they agree only in special cases, if at all. The project
should seek small examples that expose the difference.
