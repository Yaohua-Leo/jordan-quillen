# 03. Abelianization

For absolute Jordan algebras, the expected abelian objects are Jordan algebras
with zero multiplication. The resulting left-adjoint candidate is

```text
Q(J) = J / J^2.
```

This is different from Lie abelianization because Jordan algebras are already
commutative.

## Research Tasks

- Prove that zero multiplication objects are the abelian objects in the
  absolute category.
- Prove that `Q` is left adjoint to the inclusion of vector spaces as
  zero-multiplication Jordan algebras.
- Extend the discussion to relative abelianization in a slice category.
