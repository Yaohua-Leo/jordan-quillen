# CLAIM-0005: Transitivity Triangle

Status: conjectural

Statement:
For morphisms `A -> B -> C` of Jordan algebras, the Jordan cotangent complex
should satisfy a transitivity triangle analogous to Quillen's triangle for
commutative rings.

Dependencies:
- `CLAIM-0004-universal-jordan-differentials.md`
- `theory/06-cotangent-complex.md`
- `theory/07-transitivity-and-base-change.md`

Proof sketch:
Choose compatible cofibrant or simplicial resolutions and compare universal
differentials through the composite `A -> B -> C`.

References:
- `literature/notes/quillen-1970.md`

Verification notes:
No proof yet. The exact hypotheses depend on the resolution category and
Jordan module flatness.
