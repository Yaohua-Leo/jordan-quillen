# CLAIM-0001: Zero Multiplication Abelian Objects

Status: draft

Statement:
In the category of Jordan algebras over `k`, the abelian objects are expected
to be exactly the Jordan algebras with zero multiplication.

Dependencies:
- `theory/00-conventions.md`
- `theory/03-abelianization.md`

Proof sketch:
Show that the internal abelian group structure forces product operations to be
additive homomorphisms compatible with the zero object, then check that zero
multiplication Jordan algebras carry the required abelian object structure.

References:
- `literature/notes/on-homology-of-jordan-algebras.md`
