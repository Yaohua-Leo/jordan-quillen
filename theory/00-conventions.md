# 00. Conventions

Default assumptions until explicitly changed:

- The base field is `k`.
- Characteristic is zero unless a claim says otherwise.
- Jordan algebras are commutative, generally nonassociative `k`-algebras.
- The Jordan product is written by juxtaposition or `x * y`.
- `J^2` denotes the linear span of all products `xy`.
- Chain-complex and simplicial conventions are provisional and must be fixed
  before any theorem depending on signs is marked proved.

Status vocabulary:

- `draft`: working text, not yet checked.
- `conjectural`: mathematically plausible but not proved here.
- `source-linked`: backed by a cited source.
- `proved`: proved in this repository.
